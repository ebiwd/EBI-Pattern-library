DNA & RNA services

EMBL-EBI facilitates 12 DNA & RNA services, tools, and projects.

The European Bioinformatics Institute (EMBL-EBI) maintains the world’s most comprehensive range of freely available and up-to-date molecular databases. Learn more
Databases

ArrayExpress

ArrayExpress Archive of Functional Genomics Data stores data from high-throughput functional genomics experiments, and provides data for reuse to the research community.
View service

Ensembl

Ensembl enables and advances genome science by providing high-quality, integrated annotation on vertebrate genomes within a consistent and accessible infrastructure.
View service

Clustal Omega

Multiple sequence alignment of DNA or protein sequences. Clustal Omega replaces the older ClustalW alignment tools.
View service

European Nucleotide Archive

An open, supported platform for the management, sharing, integration, archiving and dissemination of public-domain sequence data.
View service

Analysis tools

FASTA [nucleotide]

Similarity search tool for nucleotide sequence databases.
View service

InterPro

A database for the classification of proteins into families, domains and conserved sites.
View service

KALIGN

Very fast multiple sequence alignment tool, suitable for large alignments.
View service

Project websites

European Variation Archive

The EVA is an open-access database of all types of genetic variation data, from all species. The EVA provides access to highly detailed, granular, raw variant data from human, with other species to follow.
View service

RNAcentral

A database of non-coding RNA sequences.
View service
