Search in local masthead

You may place your service search in the local masthead using this pattern. This is the most widely used approach, if you do so be sure to include the id #local-search on your form element.

When your page contains an element with the id of #local-search or #ebi_search, the class .no-global-search will be added to the body tag and the black bar (gloabl-masthead) search will be disabled, as has happened on this page.
