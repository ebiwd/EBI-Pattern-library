Favicons

This template preloads the EMBL-EBI mark, supporting a range of browser tab icons, bookmarks, home screen icons, tab colours, and size/formats, including SVG.

If you need to generate a new set of favicons, we've found RealFaviconGenerator.net to generate a high quality output, and be easy to use.
